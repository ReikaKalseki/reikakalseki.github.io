Overview

Factor-I/O adds a number of sensors meant to be used as inputs to circuit control logic, to enable kinds and manners of automation not normally possible.

Sensor List

The sensors added by Factor-I/O are:
  • Empty Slot Counter
  • Fluid Temperature Sensor
  • Research Progress Monitor
  • Daytime Sensor (detects exact time of day)
  • Timer (pulses at a fixed rate)
  • Nearby enemy detector (counts nearby enemies)
  • Power production monitor
  • Power consumption monitor
  • Power satisfaction monitor
  • Logistic bot counter - counts bots available to the entire network
  • Construction bot counter - counts bots available to the entire network
  • Global train status monitor - reports total count of moving, parked, waiting, and lost trains
  • Train fill sensor - reports how many connected train stops have empty/full trains

Actuators

There is also an actuator in the mod, ie a sensor that actually changes something. This is the inserter filter actuator, which will change inserter filters as it is directed to do so.
