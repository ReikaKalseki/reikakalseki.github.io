General

ScaledOres makes high-tier planets spawn a bit more of the valuable nontiered ores (gold, diamond, core fragments, etc), than lower-tier ones, with increasing rate at increasing depth. This makes deep diving on high-tier planets like magma worlds worthwhile, as they offer better rewards than can simply be gotten on the starter garden worlds.
