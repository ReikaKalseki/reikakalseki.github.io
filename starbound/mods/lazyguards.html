General

LazyGuards removes villages' guards thief pursuit and attack behavior, sometimes because you REALLY want those durasteel beds or crystal lamps, and sometimes because you are just sick of being blamed for villages disintegrating in fire rain.

There are also new "voice" lines for the guards and NPCs to reflect the guards' new passivity and lack of care for the villagers' pleas for help.
