General

This mod overhauls moon generation to make exploring them more rewarding and interesting, while keeping a vanilla feel. It adds several new biomes, expands the ore profile, and in general adds more variety and value, particularly to greater depths. In addition to new ores, it makes erchius spawns gradually denser the deeper you go.

Biomes

Blasted

This surface biome is a cratered wasteland, covered in meteor remnants. As a result, it also has a high quantity of metal ores, including some not found elsewhere.

Carbonaceous

Deposits of organic material are common on the surface here, kept warm by tar and a small amount of lava.

Junkyard

The surface here is littered with debris and waste, used as a dumping ground in some distant past.

Ice Caves

These shallow caves are mostly devoid of ores, but small pools of liquid water are common.

Shadow Caves

Just beneath the surface in some areas, a few shiny things here and there dot jet-black caverns.

Erchius Sea

At a mid-level depth, you can find massive seams of liquid erchius.

Geode

Deep underground in this region, the terrain consists of lots of obsidian and magmarock shells, some still full of lava. Valuable gems are found here.

Crystal Caves

These deep caves are rocky but abundant with glittering crystal.

Metallic Core

Some moons will have a core studded with the sunken remains of ancient meteoritic bombardment; this rare find is very rich in both metals or crystal erchius.
