General

Cryopathy is a work-in-progress cryoplasm maniplation mod, which makes some tweaks to cryoplasm behavior and enables the application of cryoplasm co-opted from the spawners found in the cold caverns during Frozen Factory.

Aside from the fact that it makes cryoplasm avoid ore and never overwrite machinery, its current primary feature centers around the ability to harvest cryoplasm from the spawners and dump that into the magma caverns, violently clearing the magma and the worms that spawn there. This is done by mixing the remains of destroyed cryoplasm - with some mechanisms being more amenable to leaving this behind than others which might burn so hot as to all but vaporize it - with freezon and organic matter to make a placeable cryoplasm analogue.

Configuration Settings

This mod contains several config options to modify values and behaviors - such as for balance or server safety concerns, or simply personal taste - to your preferences. Note that several settings may have bounds imposed on their values, either logically or explicitly; these can usually be seen in the actual settings files or ingame. These are listed in order found in the source code, which is generally the order in which they were added; They may be present in a different order ingame or in the configuration files.
The settings for the mod, when installed, can be found in the ModSettings/ReikaKalseki subfolder of your FCE userdata folder (typically AppData/Local/ProjectorGames).
