General

This mod can make various small and medium changes to various aspects of the game, primarily to add QoL changes or minor bug fixes, or to rebalance specific properties. Overall, the game progression remains unchanged, and most of the significant or balance-changing options can be disabled.

Details

The full list of changes is as follows:
  • You can now click through the top half of hydroponics bays to the plants below
  • Adds an item magnet to increase item collection range
  • Gas compressors now run faster if more than 50% full of power, with increasing speed as they approach 100%
    Configurable
  • Particle compressors now have higher yields if more than 50% full of power, with increasing speed as they approach 100%, but this increases PPS usage
    Configurable
  • Geothermal well shaft digging can now penetrate magmacite and other ore below 1050m that blocks it from reaching the lava
    Configurable
  • Motor belts can now pull directly from Continuous Casting Basins (helps fix "overload jamming" that can result from stack size mods)
  • Mk3 grapple gun is now full speed in ALL caverns, not just the cold one
    Configurable
  • Hive brains are now producible from recombined organic matter at a cost (by default) of 60 units
    Configurable
  • Being able to craft several equivalent-cost blocks and multiblock components (eg blast furnace and casting basin, resin harvesting blocks, airlocks, or minecart track pieces) between each other
  • Energy Grommet and Logistics Grommet now permit LPTs and MMs respectively
  • Energy Grommet and Logistics Grommet blocks no longer count as glass for room-definition purposes
  • Magma bore power cost now scales with difficulty settings: halved on casual or -25% on "easy" resources setting; an additional halving for rush mode, and an additional -20% for "easy power" setting
    Configurable
  • Freight carts now have a 40/tick (default) loading throughput vs the original 5 (which is scarcely better than minecarts)
    Configurable
  • Ampules can now be uncrafted back to seeds
    Configurable
  • Unused research pods can be converted into useful items
    Configurable
  • Outer airlocks are optionalTOOLTIP:gear|Configurable}
  • Rebalanced mattermitter range dropoffs by tier
    Configurable
  • Increased casting pipe range
    Configurable
  • Hive brain now yields 25 research points instead of 10
  • Eliminates worm jump scares by increasing reveal range
    Configurable
  • Prevent item despawns from age
    Configurable
  • Add a recipe to produce geothermal blocks from heimal blocks rather than the chromed and magnetic blocks (one each of which make a heimal block), for a slight (10%) discount
    Configurable
  • MadVandal's logistics falcors can now be downgraded from T2 to T1
  • Conduits can go through energy grommets aligned with their central axis
  • Greatly increased sharing from high-tier to low-tier PSBs, to help keep the latter full if the former is
    Configurable
  • Boosted power throughput on things like conduits and induction plates
    Configurable
  • Increase GAC speed when fully powered
    Configurable
  • Increased smelting speed for higher tiers of forced induction, not just collection speed
    Configurable
  • Increased item capacity for basic minecarts when used as freight
    Configurable
  • Cheaper ARC smelter upgrades
    Configurable
  • Unlockable Mk3 gun at the beginning of FF
    Configurable
  • Eliminated FALCOR beacon sky requirement
    Configurable
  • Eliminated item despawn due to aging
    Configurable
  • Disabling the use of Magma music in post-FF cold caverns
    Configurable
  • Reduced output buffer of fuel compressor to (default) 10 HOF
    Configurable
  • Changed T2 lift recipe to use alloyed instead of ultimate modules (saving that for T3), but doubling the count
    Configurable

Configuration Settings

This mod contains several config options to modify values and behaviors - such as for balance or server safety concerns, or simply personal taste - to your preferences. Note that several settings may have bounds imposed on their values, either logically or explicitly; these can usually be seen in the actual settings files or ingame. These are listed in order found in the source code, which is generally the order in which they were added; They may be present in a different order ingame or in the configuration files.
The settings for the mod, when installed, can be found in the ModSettings/ReikaKalseki subfolder of your FCE userdata folder (typically AppData/Local/ProjectorGames).
