Overview

This section of the site contains information, downloads, and additional resources for my Minecraft mods.

Every mod has its own page, in addition to a number of pages for general information pertaining to questions, changelogs, policy information, or modpack lists.

Important Notices

FML ID Patch For Uninstalling

If you remove DragonAPI from your world without first installing an FML patch (here), your modded block IDs will become scrambled and the world will be unusable!
You can remove the patch after you have loaded the world once with it installed.

1.6.4 Update URL Fix

For those using the 1.6.4 versions, the original online version file was taken down, which causes the game to 404 and take a much longer time to load.
To fix this, install this small mod here.

Content

The following is a full list of my mods for Minecraft, sorted alphabetically:

Mods

  • DragonAPI - The core mod to all my Minecraft mods.
  • ArchiSections - Adds the ability to section your base into different rendering volumes to improve FPS.
  • CaveControl - Allows for extensive customization of cave generation.
  • ChromatiCraft - A very large and deep magic mod with extensive exploration, visual, and progression elements.
  • CondensedOres - Adds the ability for fully customized ore generation profiles.
  • CritterPet - Allows for the taming of many creatures from vanilla and mods.
  • DyeTrees - Adds a tree for each dye color, and which drops those on harvest.
  • ElectriCraft - Adds a new way of transporting and distributing RotaryCraft power.
  • EnderForest - Adds a new biome where ender-related materials are abundant.
  • Expanded Redstone - Adds a variety of redstone blocks to make redstone more useful in a modded environment or for simple machines.
  • GeoStrata - Adds many different rock types and variations thereof to allow for much more varied building. Also adds subterranean vents.
  • LegacyCraft - Adds the ability to restore many aspects of older versions of the game.
  • LootTweaks - Allows for extensive reconfiguration of loot tables.
  • MeteorCraft - Adds rare ore-loaded meteor impactors.
  • ReactorCraft - Expands upon RotaryCraft with realistic nuclear reactors and power generation from them.
  • RotaryCraft - A very large tech mod focused on rotational shaft power, realistic physical laws, and with a very large complement of machines.
  • Satisforestry - Adds a biome recreating the pink forest from Satisfactory.
  • TerritoryZone - Allows for the demarcation of territories on the map to warn and optionally restrict players who do not own the area.
  • TreeClimbing - Adds the ability to climb trees.
  • UsefulTNT - Adds a tool to detonate TNT safely and with a 100% drop rate.
  • Void Monster - Adds eerie ambience to the deep world...and more.
