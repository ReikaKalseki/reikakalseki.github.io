General

This is the version of my Anaconda that I use in my survival environment.

This version has a number of added features, including a built-in Nanite plant, more ore processing capability, a grinder/salvage bay on the underside, more balanced power generation, nicer, more navigable internal passages, a full parachute deploy system, welders on the front, a 'broadside' rocket attack on each side, and an emissive paint skin and Anaconda ship kit (imitated from the ones in E:D, as can be seen here). It is also significantly faster and more agile, has more defensive measures, more storage capacity, and has a little more in the way of utility features. Its interior looks largely the same; most of its changes are external parts like thrusters, weapons, or paint.

Accomplishing this required mods! If you want an Anaconda in vanilla, see the link to my vanilla version above.

Also note that the emissive paint (which was faked with lights, as I know of no emissive-armor or emissive-paint mods) does not show well in screenshots, as it changes very dynamically.
