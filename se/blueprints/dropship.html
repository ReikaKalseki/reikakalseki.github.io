General

This started as ShinobiDraca's dropship blueprint, but I have vastly improved its functionality. The external appearance is almost completely unchanged, especially from above or the front, but the interior has been completely gutted and redesigned to massively enhance its utility, without requiring any mods.

This version of the ship has more power production, more speed and agility, more armament and durability, a longer jump range, cleaner internal systems, more maintenance features (eg medical and projector bays), more gas storage, and more automated control systems (see my scripts library for those control scripts). It also is fully pressurized and oxygen-compatible.
