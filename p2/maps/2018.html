Maps - 2018

These are my 2018 maps, listed in reverse chronological order (ie newest first).

I got much better at mapping between 2017 and 2018, and some improvements in BEEMod allowed for chambers that were both more functional and more aesthetically pleasing. This is also when I started experimenting with Mel-style "two stage" chambers, or chambers that reconfigured themselves during play.
