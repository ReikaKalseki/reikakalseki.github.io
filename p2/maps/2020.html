Maps - 2020

These are my 2020 maps, listed in reverse chronological order (ie newest first).

This was my first year making major use of Hammer to create custom BEEMod items, starting simple with a small pure-geometry item intended for Organized Chaos (which never even ended up getting used for it), but rapidly grew to large sections of levels, and including many functional programmatic elements that were relevant to the solutions.

Redshift

Some of the "spillover" content from Blue And Orange, adapted into an independent, complete, moderate-difficulty puzzle. This is also my first ever use of the inquisition field. Also features major Hammer work, probably more by both quantity and complexity than I had ever attempted by that point. The core concept of this map actually dates back to 2018, where it started small, grew overly complex, was abandoned, revisited a year later, redesigned, abandoned again, and finally revisited and completed in 2020.
