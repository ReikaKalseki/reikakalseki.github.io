Maps - 2017 Post-BEE

These are my 2017 Post-BEE maps, listed in reverse chronological order (ie newest first).

Early in 2017 was the first time I started using BEEMod to expand the capabilities of the PeTI editor. While I never went overboard with the new tools - aside from perhaps a couple gimmick maps - I did make full use of its tools to both streamline and enrich my maps.
