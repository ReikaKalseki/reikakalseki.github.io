Overview

FastFurnaces adds a higher tier of many mainline production machines, including:
  • Electric Furnace
  • Chemical Plant
  • Refinery
  • Offshore Pump
  • Small Pump
  • Long Inserter
  • Mining Drill
  • Beacons
  • Chests
  • BobMods solar and steam

Every feature is indivdually controllable in the settings.

Special Machines

In most cases, these upgraded versions are simply more expensive but more powerful (usually faster, hence the name). However, a few upgrades are special. In particular, enhanced mining drills are actually far more efficient and clean compared to their base counterparts, without being hugely faster. Beacons actually have two additional tiers, each gaining module slots, range, and transmission efficiency (and extreme power cost).

Modules

FastFurnaces also adds T4 and T5 modules. These, especially the production modules, are generally unlimited compared to vanilla ones, and can get stupidly overpowered, to the point where the base game had a patch to prevent numerical overflow caused by stacking them.

Configuration Settings

This mod contains several config options to modify values and behaviors - such as for balance or server safety concerns, or simply personal taste - to your preferences. Note that several settings may have bounds imposed on their values, either logically or explicitly; these can usually be seen in the actual settings files or ingame. These are listed in order found in the source code, which is generally the order in which they were added; They may be present in a different order ingame or in the configuration files.

Extend BobPower

Type: bool
Current Default Value:
true
This enables or disables the late-tier solars and steam engines to extend bobpower. Does nothing without that mod installed.

Unrestricted productivity modules

Type: bool
Current Default Value:
false
This makes the Tier-5 productivity modules have no restrictions on their use.

Tier 4+ Modules

Type: bool
Current Default Value:
