Feedback and Issues

If you want to contact me over something pertaining to the mods, the best place to do so depends on the nature of what you wish to say; in particular, bug reports are generally best sent to a system dedicated to handling them exclusively.

Bug Reports

Bug reports should be reported on the GitHub issue tracker; this allows them to be referenced over a longer period of time, cross-linked, tagged with appropriate labels, put on hold when waiting for ideas or information, and shelved when solved or abandoned.
The issue tracker can be found here: https://github.com/ReikaKalseki/Reika_Mods_Issues/issues

Please do not use the issue tracker for anything except bug reports; it is not well-suited for long back-and-forth discussions, and is not nearly as well-travelled as the discussion forums.

Feature Discussions

For discussions about other things, including asking for information, pack permission requests, making feature suggestions, giving feedback about design, or more, this is best conducted on one of the other platforms, in particular the Minecraft forums or on Discord. These can be accessed from the appropriate icon in the link row in the page footer.

Please do not use email as a way of contacting me, unless you have no other options! It is much less likely to be noticed quickly and much more of a hassle to navigate.
If you like any of my mods, maps, or other content, or want to help me develop them, feel free to use my Patreon or the buttons below. Every little bit helps me be able to keep working on them, and helps ensure both maintenance and expansion of them.
If you wish to give a one-time donation, I recommend you use the Paypal link on the right; Patreon, though it can be used for one-time donations, is designed for ongoing monthly 'subscriptions' (ie a set amount of money gifted each month for as long as you remain a 'patron'). Paypal can also register a donation with monthly recurrence, should that seem preferable to you.
For both systems, the amount you choose to donate is up to you, and for Patreon, can be changed at any time should you wish to decrease or increase the amount.
And thank you for your support. :D
